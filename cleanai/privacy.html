Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

At Clean AI, we believe your photos are your business, not ours. Our privacy policy is built on the principle of data minimization.

The Core Policy: We do not upload, collect, or store your photos on any external servers. All AI analysis for similarity and duplicate detection is performed locally on your device.

1. Information We Access

To function, the app requires permission to access your Photo Library. This access is used strictly for scanning, sorting, and deleting files per your command.

2. Data Usage

  • Photo Data: Stays on your iPhone. We never see your images.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., crash reports, feature usage) to improve the app. This does not include personal identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use standard Apple services for iCloud syncing (if enabled) and App Store processing. Please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Your Rights

You can revoke photo access at any time through your iPhone Settings. Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to "delete" upon request.
